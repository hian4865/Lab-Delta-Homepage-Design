Elephant Bookstore

Elephant Bookstore, a locally owned and entirely independent bookstore, was established in 2004 to offer an affirming space to people of all kinds. Any sort of bookworm, teacher, student, and enthusiast can find respite in the halls of this bookstore and lose themselves in the store’s curated collection of books. With a strong sense of community, the Elephant Bookstore is a landmark for all to come and find shelter in.
